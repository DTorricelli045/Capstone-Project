Join the Movement

Have old parts sitting around the house or at work? Our services will help you remove unused parts by taking them of your hands. The US itself produces more e-waste than any other country. The amount of parts that are thrown away every year is about 9.4 million tons of E-waste. This impacts our environment because of the toxic materials found in electronics that can affect any living being that is around them. About 200 million people worldwide are being exposed and at risk from E-waste.

Mission

Improving the environment by accepting old parts that are not used and repurposing and reusing them to give old parts a second life while taking the steps to making the world E-waste free.

Vision

Our vision is to impact the world by providing our services all over America to stop E-waste production with the help of others that want to make a positive change to our ecosystems.
